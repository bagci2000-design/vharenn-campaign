**Player-Facing Lore:**  
Dorrim is the patron of artisans, merchants, and builders. His worship focuses on the creation of lasting works and the sanctity of fair deals. His temples double as trade halls and forges, their walls covered in etched contracts. His symbol is a hammer striking an anvil over a stone tablet.
